Explore the natural Habitats of Indian Birds

wetlands

Wetlands are home to migratory birds like flamingo and cranes.

Forests

Dense forests shelter birds like the great Indian Hornbill and peafowl.

Grassland

Grassland are home to birds like the Indian Bustard and harriers.

Himalayas

High-altitude birds like the Himalayan Monal thrive in this region.

coasts

Seabirds like gulls and terns and commonly found along India's coasts.
